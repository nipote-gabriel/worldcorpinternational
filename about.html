About WorldCorp International

Our Mission

Tired of evil corporations fucking up your life? Sick of business news only talking about Tesla? Feel like you don’t actually know anything about how the world works? Who is building the bridges? Can the internet break? Is somebody gonna solve world hunger? That’s why we built WorldCorp International. We look like a multi-national evil conglomerate, but we’re actually exposing their bullshit, and shining the spotlight on social entrepreneurs — the people doing the real work in the world. Maybe if you follow our stuff, you’ll learn enough to help do something good.

Our Approach

Founded by an experienced entrepreneur and standup comedian, we take an honest look at business and entrepreneurship. We interview folks working on the most interesting projects that actually help people. Our hope is that if we shine a light on people putting in work on good projects, they’ll gain more momentum, attract more talent, and have an easier time succeeding.

Join Us

Subscribe to our YouTube Channel and Newsletter and follow us on socials to stay connected. We’re based in NYC and regularly throw live events aimed at making progress while having fun.

Quick Facts
